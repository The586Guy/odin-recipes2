No Bake Protein Cookie Dough Recipe

Home

Description

This healthy, protein-packed cookie dough is capable of conquering any sweet cravings that may sneak up on you! No baking required and ready in just 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt (add more as needed)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar free maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla protein powder
  • 3-4 tablespoons oat flower or almond flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons choice of mix-ins (chocolate chips, M&Ms, peanut butter nibs, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl mix together peanut butter, Greek yogurt, maple syrup (and vanilla extract if using) until smooth.
  2. To the same bowl, stir in protein powder, oat flour, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Add mix-ins and stir again. Place in the freezer for about 5 minutes to chill and firm up. If you notice the consistency is too thin you can increase the amount of oat flour a tablespoon at a time until your desired consistency is reached.
  4. Bon apetit!
